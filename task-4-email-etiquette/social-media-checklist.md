DO’S (The Green Flags)
1. Audit your privacy settings to ensure only friends see your personal photos and locations.
2. Network with professionals and alumni in the AI and CS fields to build your future career.
3. Fact-check before sharing news or articles to avoid spreading digital misinformation.
4. Showcase your projects, such as GitHub repos or AI models, to build a "digital portfolio."
5. Use "Two-Factor Authentication" (2FA) to keep your accounts secure from being hacked.

DON’TS (The Red Flags)
1. Don't post in the heat of the moment or when you’re feeling highly emotional or angry.
2. Don't share sensitive data like your hostel room number, student ID, or daily schedule.
3. Don't engage with "trolls" or participate in online harassment/cyberbullying.
4. Don't post content that violates college policy or shows illegal activities.
5. Don't plagiarize others' code or creative work and claim it as your own.
